Acquiring a Thilyarem

Your official guide to obtaining a Thilyarem.

The Hatchery: Your First Thilyarem

The primary and recommended method for new participants. As a new member, you are allowed to hatch one common egg to begin your adventure!

Visit the Hatchery

The Adoption Center

A secondary avenue for acquiring a Thilyarem. All members may adopt up to two Thilyarem from the center. This policy may be adjusted to reflect supply and demand at a later date.

Go to the Adoption Center

Player Trading & Sales

Obtaining genos and breeding slots directly from other players. Trades often involve art, USD, or in-game items. Sale advertisements are most frequent on our official Discord.

Join the Discord

Breeding

For those seeking custom or non-standard genos. Acquire breeding slots via player exchanges or by purchasing starter slots during official sales events.

Learn About Breeding
