Lined

Common Marking

LL nL

Appearance

Lined is part of these marking combinations: Tabby, Mystery, Runic

Lined creates horizontal striping which flows with the contours of the body.

Requirements

Lined consists of at least one horizontal stripe within its designated range. It must be a single, solid colour unless affected by a colour modifier.
